Comprehensive Blood Checkup Services

Introduction

At Physio DietFit Care, we provide trusted and affordable blood checkup services to support your health and wellness journey. Our expert team ensures accuracy, confidentiality, and patient comfort through every step of testing.

From routine health checkups to advanced laboratory diagnostics, we help you track your well-being with professional care and home collection options.

Services Offered

Urine Analysis

Comprehensive urine tests to detect infections, kidney function, and overall health status.

Microbiology Testing

Advanced tests for bacterial, viral, and fungal infections to support accurate diagnosis.

Pathology Services

Routine blood tests, liver & kidney function, thyroid, cholesterol, and more.

Genetic Testing

DNA-based tests to identify inherited conditions, health risks, and personalized care needs.

How It Works

  1. Book Appointment: Schedule your test online or via phone.
  2. Sample Collection: Choose home collection or visit our nearest lab.
  3. Lab Testing: Our experts perform accurate and timely analysis.
  4. Reports & Consultation: Receive your results securely with expert guidance.
