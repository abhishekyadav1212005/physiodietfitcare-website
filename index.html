About Us

A healthcare initiative founded by professionals from PGIMER Chandigarh that integrates physiotherapy, nutritional care, and diagnostic testing to deliver a personalized and preventive approach to fitness and recovery. Our process begins with a Comprehensive Health Assessment (CBC, Vitamin, Lipid, etc.), helping us identify key deficiencies and biomarkers that often go unnoticed. Based on these insights, our team recommends a customized diet plan — and if needed, essential supplements — to restore internal balance. To complete the cycle, our physiotherapy guidance focuses on targeted exercises and mobility correction to improve function, reduce pain, and eliminate weakness. Whether you're recovering post-illness, working toward peak performance, or simply aiming to feel better every day — Physio DietFit Care is here to guide you with science-backed wellness that actually works.
